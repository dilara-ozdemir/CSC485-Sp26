# The Unsolved Case

## The Discovery
Late one stormy night, something unusual was found in the old Bakers house. It was a dark place, abandoned for years. 

## The Witnesses
A group of young kids were walking by the Bakers home and noticed two figures running away in the forest, but didn't think much of it originally.

## Clues 
Objects, notes, and minor inconsistencies began to appear. Some were real, some misleading.

## The Suspects
A few individuals seem connected to the events, though their roles are unclear.

## The Twist
Just when things seemed straightforward, new evidence complicated the case.

## Investigation
Decisions must be made about which clues to follow and which to ignore.

## The Outcome
The mystery is far from resolved. What will happen next?
